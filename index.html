I am a Research Fellow at the University of Edinburgh's Usher Institute, and part of the DARTH research group, lead by Associate Prof. Athanasios Tsanas. My research, and its application into asthma care, is supported by the Asthma UK Centre for Applied Research (AUKCAR).

My primary research interests include:

  • Machine Learning Classification Models for Medical Prognostic Modelling
  • Data Extraction from Electronic Health Records
  • Estimating Medication Adherence from Primary Care Prescription Records
